  • Behavioral and technical pre-screening
  • Reference and background coordination
  • Offer negotiation and onboarding support
  • Placement guarantee included

Retained Search

Exclusive. Dedicated. Guaranteed.

For your most critical hires — C-suite, VP, and confidential replacements — our retained model gives you an exclusive partnership, a dedicated search team, and a structured process built around your company's specific needs.

  • ✓ Exclusive engagement — we work for you alone on this search
  • ✓ Dedicated search team with a structured weekly cadence
  • ✓ Deep market mapping — active and passive candidates
  • ✓ Confidential searches handled with full discretion
  • ✓ Full replacement guarantee
Start a Retained Search
⭐

Best For

  • CFO, VP Finance, and C-suite placements
  • Confidential or backfill searches
  • Hard-to-fill or niche finance roles
  • Private equity and high-growth companies
  • Searches requiring market intel and benchmarking

Retainer paid in installments — a portion upfront, balance upon placement. Full replacement guarantee included.
Contract-to-Hire

Try Before You Hire

Start a candidate on contract and convert them to a full-time employee when you're confident in the fit. This model reduces risk for both sides and is increasingly popular in today's market.

  • Typical trial period of 90–180 days
  • Candidate works on your team from day one
  • Conversion fee structure agreed upfront
  • Ideal when headcount approval is in progress
  • Candidate gains valuable company exposure
Explore Contract-to-Hire
🔄

Best For

  • Roles where culture fit is critical
  • New positions with evolving scope
  • Candidates seeking a foot in the door
  • Companies with hiring freezes in process
  • High-volume accounting teams
Fee Structure
Bill rate on contract; reduced conversion fee upon permanent hire
