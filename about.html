About Staff Financial Group

A boutique finance and accounting recruiting firm built on expertise, relationships, and results — serving Atlanta and the Southeast since 2005.

Our Story

Built Around One Simple Belief

Great organizations are built by great finance teams. Staff Financial Group exists to help both sides of that equation find each other — faster, smarter, and with more confidence than a generalist staffing firm can offer.

We focus exclusively on finance and accounting recruiting because we believe true expertise in a single domain produces dramatically better outcomes. When you call us, you're speaking with someone who knows the difference between a consolidating Controller and a hands-on one — and who knows exactly which candidates belong in each seat.

Headquartered in Atlanta, we serve clients and candidates throughout the Southeast and beyond, placing professionals at every level — from Staff Accountants to Chief Financial Officers.

Our Values

What Guides Every Search

We hold ourselves to the same standard we expect from the candidates we represent — honesty, rigor, and a genuine commitment to long-term outcomes over short-term transactions.

  • Rigorous vetting — we never send a candidate we wouldn't personally vouch for
  • Honest communication — no spin, no over-promising, even when it's not what you want to hear
  • Long-term relationships — we measure success in years of repeat business, not individual placements
  • Market transparency — comp data, talent availability, and hiring reality shared up front
  • Responsiveness — you'll never wait days for an update; we treat your timeline like our own
