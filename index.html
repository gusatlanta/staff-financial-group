Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What types of finance professionals does Staff Financial Group place? +
We place CFOs, VPs of Finance, Controllers, Accounting Managers, Senior and Staff Accountants, Financial Analysts, FP&A professionals, Tax Managers, Internal Auditors, Cost Accountants, Treasury Analysts, Payroll Managers, and AP/AR specialists — exclusively finance and accounting roles, across all industries.
How quickly will I receive a shortlist of candidates? +
We deliver a blinded shortlist of pre-vetted candidates within 48 to 72 hours of receiving your talent request. Our active pipeline and deep Atlanta network allow us to move faster than generalist staffing firms.
Do you offer contract, temp, or interim staffing? +
Yes. We provide contract, temp-to-hire, and interim staffing for all finance and accounting roles — from Interim CFO and Controller to contract Accountants and ERP implementation support. We act as the Employer of Record, so payroll, taxes, workers' comp, and compliance are fully handled on our end.
What does "blinded shortlist" mean? +
A blinded profile shows a candidate's skills, experience, title, and location — but no name, employer name, or contact information. This protects candidate privacy during the screening phase. Once you express interest, we facilitate the introduction.
Where does Staff Financial Group place candidates? +
We are headquartered in Atlanta, GA, and primarily serve companies throughout Atlanta and the Southeast. We have placed finance and accounting professionals in Charlotte, Nashville, Tampa, and other major Southeast markets, and handle remote searches nationally.
